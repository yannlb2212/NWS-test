About the NBA

The National Basketball Association (NBA) is a professional basketball league in the United States, comprising 30 teams divided into two conferences: the Eastern Conference and the Western Conference. Founded in 1946, it is widely regarded as the premier men's professional basketball league in the world.

Star Players

The NBA has been home to some of the greatest basketball players of all time, including Michael Jordan, Magic Johnson, Kareem Abdul-Jabbar, and current stars like Giannis Antetokounmpo, Stephen Curry, and Kevin Durant.
